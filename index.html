Equivalence in Translation Challenge

Translate the following culturally loaded texts into another language. Try both a literal translation and a functional (pragmatic/culturally adapted) translation.

1. Idiom

English: "Spill the beans"

2. Joke/Pun

English: "Why did the scarecrow win an award? Because he was outstanding in his field!"

3. Political Slogan

English: "Make America Great Again"

4. Brand Slogan

English: "Just Do It" (Nike)
